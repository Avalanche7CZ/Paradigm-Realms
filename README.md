# Paradigm Realms

Give every player a permanent place to call home, while keeping the world outside fresh and full of adventure.

Paradigm Realms is a server-side mod for creating protected personal realms from configurable presets, alongside a shared Wilds world designed for exploration and periodic resets.

**Players do not need Paradigm Realms installed on their client.**

---

## Features

- 🌍 Permanent personal realms
- 🏡 Protected private bases
- 👥 Members, visitors, and access controls
- ✨ Configurable realm presets
- 📦 Schematic and structure imports
- 🛡️ Built-in realm protection
- 🌲 Shared exploration-focused Wilds world
- 🎲 Safe random teleportation
- 🔄 Crash-safe Wilds reset workflow
- 💾 Persistent realm data
- 🔌 Optional Paradigm integration
- 🖥️ Fully server-side

---

## Your own little realm

Every player can create their own protected realm, a permanent space for building, storage, farms, machines, or anything else they would rather not lose during an exploration-world reset.

Realms include:

- Owners, members, and visitors
- Public or private access
- Invitations and member management
- Safe teleportation
- Persistent spawn points
- Protection from unauthorized building and interaction

Keep it private, invite your friends, or open it for visitors. Your realm, your rules :3

---

## Realm presets

New realms are created from configurable server-defined presets.

Servers can use built-in starter layouts or import their own islands, plots, platforms, and bases.

Supported import formats include:

- Sponge `.schem`
- Legacy WorldEdit/MCEdit `.schematic`
- Litematica `.litematic`
- Vanilla structure `.nbt`

Imported presets are validated before use, keeping placement contained within the assigned realm and preventing unsafe content from slipping through.

---

## The Wilds

The Wilds are a shared world made for exploration, resources, structures, mobs, and temporary adventures.

Unlike personal realms, the Wilds can be regenerated without affecting permanent player bases.

This makes Paradigm Realms a natural fit for:

- Survival servers
- Cobblemon servers
- Large modpacks
- Technical servers
- Community SMPs
- Servers with renewable resource worlds

Build safely at home, then wander off into the Wilds and cause problems responsibly.

---

## Built-in protection

Realm boundaries and access rules are enforced directly by the server.

Protection covers common interactions such as:

- Breaking and placing blocks
- Containers and buckets
- Entity interactions
- Explosions
- Pistons crossing realm boundaries
- Unauthorized entry into private realms

Administrators can use an explicit session-only bypass when maintenance is required.

---

## Optional Paradigm integration

When [Paradigm](https://modrinth.com/mod/paradigm) is installed, Paradigm Realms can integrate with its:

- Permission API
- Message formatting
- Placeholder API

Paradigm is completely optional. Paradigm Realms also works as a standalone mod.

---

## Server-side only

Players do **not** need Paradigm Realms installed client-side.

They only need the normal mods required by the server or modpack. Paradigm Realms does not add custom client-side blocks, items, screens, keybinds, or networking requirements.
